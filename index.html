About

I'm a Venezuelan-born creative technologist and software architect based in Toronto. I design and ship full-stack products end to end — from the data model to the pixels.

After relocating to Canada I exited a startup in 2020 to join a quantum-computing firm, built and scaled SaaS platforms, collaborated on healthcare EHR systems, and grew a real-estate portfolio of 38 properties. When I'm not shipping code you'll find me producing music or building things at hackathons.
