02 The Journey

From Caracas to Toronto

A Venezuelan-born builder who turned curiosity into a craft — designing and shipping full-stack products end to end, from the data model to the pixels.

2015 · Caracas, Venezuela

Recognized at the FILA Festival

Honored with "Powerful Minds" — early proof that creative technology could move people, not just machines.

2016–2020 · Toronto, Canada

Smart cameras, real estate & a startup exit

Built computer-vision features and scaled the cloud platform at ZEITDice; grew a real-estate portfolio of 38 properties; earned a place on George Brown College's Dean's List three semesters running. Exited a startup in 2020.

2020 · The quantum leap

Into quantum computing

Joined a quantum-computing firm and co-invented a patented system for securely distributing compute workloads to web-capable devices — later cited by Intuit, Akamai and Verizon.

2021 → Today

Scaling SaaS & healthcare platforms

Architecting and scaling SaaS products and collaborating on behavioral-health EHR systems serving 2,200+ agencies. When not shipping code: producing music and building at hackathons.
